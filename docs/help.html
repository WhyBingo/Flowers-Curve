Note: Number of circulations required to complete a closed circe = (the least common multiple of the outer circle radius and inner circle radius) / outer circle radius
Generally, the greater their common factor, the less complicated the graph will be.

Drawing step

This is radius increment of the inner circle. Generally, there's no need to adjust this parameter, unless the line drawn becomes dotted (left picture)

You may want to decrease this value to eliminate dotted lines or to enhance quality, but the cost is the drawing time.
You can reduce the drawing time by decreasing the value of the drawing delay.
